Home

Dependencies:

  • Prefunded Metamask account

Usage:

  1. Install Metamask (How-to: https://blog.wetrust.io/how-to-install-and-use-metamask-7210720ca047)
  2. Identify wallet you want to use by clicking the extension and selecting accounts.
  3. Navigate to Listings and choose investment.
  4. Select fund towards the bottom.
  5. Choose amount to fund in project and congrats you made your first investment!

FAQ:

  • Q: How do i fund my account?
    A: Navigate to metamask and select buy. You will be directed to either deposit ether directly or a crypto exchange to purchase ether.
  • Q: How can i view the progress of my investment?
    A: Once Crowdsale is complete, an email will be sent to you account at https://ethmail.cc/ with your next steps.
  • Q: What happens if the investment goal is not reached?
    A: You will recieve an email through https://ethmail.cc/ notifying you of failure of completion and the next steps.
  • Q: How do i use Ethmail?
    A: Navigate to https://ethmail.cc/ and click to login. Your login information will be your wallet address that you used to fun.
  • Q: How do i collect my investment?
    A: Once project is completed and the house is sold, we will add a return button on the reports tab where you will be refunded your ether with additional return.

About Us

Who we are:

We are crowdfunding service dedicated to connecting home contractors with retail investors looking to broaden their investment portfolio
Contractors contact us with a project with a business plan that we post on our platform to prospective investors who can invest any amount
of ether through the ethereum blockchain. We aim to help contractors gain access to capital faster by removing conventional investing
activities while simultaneously creating more investment opportunities for retail investors.
